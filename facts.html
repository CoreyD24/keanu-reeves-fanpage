Here are 5 interesting facts about Keanu Reeves

  1. Keanu Reeves was actually born in Lebanon but he is a Canadian citizen.
  2. He got to hang out with Alice Cooper as a kid at his house.
  3. He was in a band in the early 90s called Dogstar.
  4. His breakout role was in 1989 when he played Ted in Bill and Ted's Excellent Adventure.
  5. In 1997, he turned down a role in Speed 2 in which got him blacklisted from 20th Century Fox for 10 years.

Here are some more interesting facts about Keanu Reeves in no particular order

  • His name means "cool breeze over the mountains" in Hawiian.
  • When he was 3, his father abandoned him and his family.
  • He is a big motorcycle enthusiast.
  • Despite giving up so much money to get other actors and actresses onto a movie, he is still one of the highest paid actors.
  • He performs many of his own stunts.
  • Reeves reportedly trained for 6 months in preparation for John Wick as opposed to the average 8 weeks by most actors.
